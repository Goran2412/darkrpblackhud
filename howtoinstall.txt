How to install:

Gmod local game:

Go to your Steam directory, steamapps, common, GarrysMod, garrysmod, addons, darkrp modificationmaster, lua, darkrp_modules, hudreplacement and drop the lua file there. (replacing the original)

Gmod server:

Go to the server folder, garrysmod, addons, darkrp modificationmaster, lua, darkrp_modules, hudreplacement and drop the lua file.(replacing the original)
